# Services Page Content

## 1) Territory Representation
Konative serves as your dedicated manufacturer rep team in the Pacific Northwest, delivering structured territory coverage that increases consistency and market traction. We align field activity with brand priorities, ensuring key accounts receive focused support while new opportunities are developed with intent. This approach creates stronger dealer engagement and clearer visibility into regional performance.

**What's included**
- Territory coverage planning by market segment and account priority
- Regular dealer and showroom visits with documented follow-up
- Pipeline visibility and opportunity tracking by territory
- Quarterly territory performance reviews with strategic adjustments

**Target audience**
- Manufacturers

## 2) Dealer Network Development
We help manufacturers build a healthier dealer network by identifying fit, strengthening existing relationships, and improving onboarding quality across accounts. Konative works to increase channel confidence through better communication, practical training, and market-specific support. The outcome is a dealer base that is more engaged, more informed, and better positioned to sell your products.

**What's included**
- Dealer prospecting and qualification within priority territories
- New dealer onboarding and launch coordination
- Dealer training support for product positioning and applications
- Account health check-ins and channel alignment feedback

**Target audience**
- Manufacturers

## 3) Showroom and Product Positioning Support
Konative helps brands stand out in showroom environments where comparison happens quickly and details matter. We support product presentation, merchandising strategy, and partner communication so your line is represented with clarity and credibility. For surfaces, hardscape, and outdoor living lines, this translates into better product visibility and stronger buyer confidence.

**What's included**
- Showroom placement strategy and product presentation guidance
- Messaging support tailored to regional buyer and specifier behavior
- Competitive context and positioning recommendations
- Cross-functional coordination with dealer teams and internal stakeholders

**Target audience**
- Manufacturers and Dealers

## 4) Fabrication Channel Support
For fabrication-focused product lines, Konative supports the operational side of channel growth by connecting manufacturers with fabrication partners and clarifying value at the technical level. We bridge communication between product teams, dealer accounts, and fabricators so expectations stay aligned from specification to execution. This reduces friction and improves confidence in repeat business.

**What's included**
- Fabricator relationship development across key territories
- Product education support for fabrication workflows and applications
- Communication alignment between manufacturer, dealer, and fabricator teams
- Feedback loops on installation, demand patterns, and channel challenges

**Target audience**
- Manufacturers and Dealers

## 5) Market Activation and Partner Enablement
Konative designs and executes practical market activation initiatives that help manufacturers gain traction with the right partners at the right time. We focus on actions that move dealer behavior, improve local awareness, and reinforce brand standards across the territory. Each initiative is built to support partnership growth, not one-off activity.

**What's included**
- Regional launch and campaign coordination with dealer partners
- Partner enablement assets and talking-point alignment
- Event and field activation support for outdoor living and surface categories
- Performance debriefs to refine future territory execution

**Target audience**
- Manufacturers
